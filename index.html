The Foundations

Every problem can be studied through these lenses

01

Information

What do you know? What's missing? Every problem starts with understanding what information exists.

02

Structure

How is it organized? Finding the right structure turns chaos into something workable.

03

Relations

What connects to what? Dependencies, graphs, cause and effect — meaning lives in connections.

04

Change

What transforms? Problems involve state, transitions, and dynamics over time.

05

Limits

What's impossible? Constraints aren't obstacles — they're the rules that shape good solutions.

06

Patterns

What repeats? The same structures appear across math, systems, nature, and everyday life.
