The Worldview

What this place is about

Machines of Code studies how the world becomes understandable through information, structure, relations, change, and limits—and how those ideas repeat as patterns across algorithms, systems, and learning.

Every topic follows a full arc: Intuition → Model → Reasoning → Example → Build. Philosophy stays grounded. Engineering stays principled.
